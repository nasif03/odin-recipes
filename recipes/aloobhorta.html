Aloo Bhorta

Description

potatoes good

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Thinly sliced onions
  • Dry red chilli
  • Mustard oil
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes until soft.
  2. Peel after they have cooled a bit.
  3. Roast dry red chilli until slightly burnt.
  4. Mash the potatoes by hand while mixing in all the other ingredients.
  5. Mix everything well and serve as a ball.
