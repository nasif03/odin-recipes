Dal

Description

Absolutely goated source of protein. Requires like 2 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Masoor dal
  • Basic spices probably lying around at your home anyway

Steps

  1. Wash the lentils >3 times.
  2. Pressure cook with 3 cups of water for 5 minutes.
  3. Heat up some oil on a pan.
  4. Add chopped up onions, ginger, garlic paste, spices and anything else you want.
  5. Fry it until it looks edible. Oh, and also add turmeric powder(you should have been prepared).
  6. Add the dal to the pan.
  7. Add salt and water.
  8. Boil until it looks the way your mom makes it.
  9. Top with cilantro.
